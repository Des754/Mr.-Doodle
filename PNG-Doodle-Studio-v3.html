Character Drawing Notes
  • Mista Grasruts — scruffy beard, worn shorts, singlet, thongs. Slightly stooped. Cigarette optional. Mid-scheme or scheme-gone-wrong expression.
  • Agnes — strong build, laplap skirt, hair back. Famous sospen (saucepan) prop. Stern to exasperated to quietly knowing.
  • Big Fella Chief — oversized suit jacket, briefcase, hat. Pompous posture. Large belly. Wide smile hiding shifty eyes.
  • Pikinini — small, barefoot, shorts too big. Constant curiosity. Asks what adults won't.
  • Tisa — neat dress/blouse, glasses, chalk or book. Patient warm smile. Voice of reason.
  • Maket Meri — bilum bag on back/head, market stall. Assertive, confident stance. Practical clothing.
  • Highlander — traditional wig, koteka, red-black body paint. Strong stance. Culture-clash comedy in urban settings.
